Introduction

Devin has ~7,000 tweets from 2012 - 2018 (6 years). This project analyzes all ~7,000 tweets, removing retweets and replies. The tweets are split into a bag of words where a term frequency analysis is performed to find Devin's top words. The same analysis is performed on 10 other Twitter users. We train a model to predict the user that Devin tweets most similar to in order to detect whether this can predict similar goals, aspirations, interests, etc. We also perform a Digram Markov Chain on Devin's tweets in order to predict future tweets.
